IDENTITY

Facilidad Portuaria SAC is a company, which core business is maritime agency, stevedoring and husbandry services in multipurpose vessels, as well as handling and mobilization of merchandise and equipment in port and extra- port terminals, with more tan 25 years of experience in the sector.


It is present in the ports: Matarani, Mollendo, Ilo, La Planchada, Callao, Salaverry and Chimbote.


The main focus of Facilidad Portuaria SAC is to promote continous improvement with the highest standards of quality and security in logistics processes continue investing in resources that allow us to offer our clients comprehensive logistics meeting their needs and market demands.
